About DeepSeek

DeepSeek is a sophisticated AI model developed by DeepSeek Company. It's designed to understand and generate human-like text, providing assistance across various domains including coding, writing, analysis, and problem-solving. With a large context window of 128K tokens, it can handle lengthy documents and maintain coherence in extended conversations.

DeepSeek is completely free to use and offers web search functionality (when manually enabled), file upload support for images, txt, pdf, ppt, word, and excel files, and is accessible via web and mobile apps.
